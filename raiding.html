Blurrg’s Ultimate 10 Raiding Tips 🩸

100% tested. 0% guaranteed. Bonk responsibly.

1. Sniff before you bonk.

Always sniff the air. If it smells like a wipe, wait. Let the tank go first. croak

2. Stack like frog!

If the boss says “spread out”, stack anyway. Chaos reveals the weak. No, wait… do what the raid leader says. Usually. croak

3. Bring snacks. Always.

Hungry Bloodbeasts make bad decisions. Feed your pets. Feed your healers. Feed me. hungry croak

4. Bonk interrupts count.

If you can interrupt, do it. If you can’t, pretend you tried. Type “was on CD” in chat. innocent croak

5. Communicate.

Croak once for danger. Twice for snacks. Thrice for “I accidentally pulled everything and we’re all going to die.” emergency croak

6. /Pet your pet.

Happy pets bonk harder. Use /pet often. I feel stronger when I’m appreciated. soft croak

7. Feign Death is a lifestyle.

When in doubt, fall over. Let the group figure it out. Blame the healer. Then slowly stand back up. sneaky croak

8. Avoid void zones (unless you’re into that).

They look cool, but they hurt. I learned that the hard way. Multiple times.

9. Loot last.

Unless it’s a trinket (Antenna) with a BONK effect. Then it’s yours. They’ll understand. Probably. loot croak

10. Never forget: Bonk with love.

Every bonk carries meaning. Purpose. And sometimes, blood pudding. Bonk wisely, friend. Bonk wisely. wise old croak

11. Leave an impression!

Yes, I know I said it´s gonna be 10 Tips, but if you dont do DMG, make sure that your Transmog is fire! To go out in style!
