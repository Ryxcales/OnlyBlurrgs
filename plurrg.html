About Plurrg the Magical Crawg (acording to Blurrg):

Plurrg… just popped out of a hole in reality one day. Void portal. Purple sparkles. Strange whispers. Tiny confused crawg rolling on his back. We all stared. He stared back. …then sneezed void dust. concerned croak

Personality

Plurrg is… quirky.
Not bad quirky.
Just void-quirky.
• Likes to sit and hum as little sparkles float from his horns.
• Tilts his head when he hears whispers only he can hear.
• Gets super excited when he sees butterflies. Like me! proud croak

Favorite Activities

💜 Soft lilac glow = Happy! Probably saw a butterfly.
💗 Bright pinkish purple = EXTRA happy! Belly rubs. Snacks. Good vibes.
🟣 Deep violet, low glow = Sad. Lonely. Needs cuddles.
🔴 Red-tinted pulse = Angry or scared. Maybe Blurg stole his rock again. angry croak
✨ Sparkling pop-glow = Confused existential void thoughts? We don’t know.

Fun Facts

• Smells faintly of blueberries and cosmic dread.
• Sleeps curled like a glowing shrimp.
• Baby crawglings use him as a night-light.
• Blurgette shares her rock collection with him; he never judges the rocks.
• Sometimes whispers echo around him, saying “feed the crawg.”
…We do. Immediately. Just in case. nervous croak
