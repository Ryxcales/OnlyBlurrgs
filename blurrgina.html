Oh, where do I start? Blurrgina… she’s… She’s the best. The beastest. croak

The PVP Crawg

She’s a Bloodbeast, like me, but… she’s different. Fierce. Vicious. You see a troll or some nasty creature? She’ll tear them apart without even blinking. And with Nervura by her side in PVP? Hoo… hoo… croak You don’t want to be their enemy. Nooo. Not even a little.

Personality

But… she’s also… playful. She knows I like her. I bring her snacks. Bloodpudding, sometimes rare sausage she sniffs, maybe takes it, maybe just nudges it to the babies, but sometimes she dances for me. croak She spins and twirls in the mud like it’s like it’s magic!

• Vicious: In battle, she’s relentless, calculating, and terrifying dont challenge her
• Loyal: Fiercely protective of Nervura and the crawgs under her care.
• Flirtatious & playful: She knows Blurrg’s affections and uses them to her advantage
• Intelligent: Sharp-minded, she anticipates enemy moves and manipulates the battlefield to her favor.

Favorite Activities
