About Blurg the Troublemaker (acording to Blurrg):

Blurg is my cousin. Yes, my annoying, chaotic, headbutting, tail-swinging cousin. He’s bigger, louder, and somehow always in trouble. Where I bring snacks, he eats snacks. Where I protect crawgs, he scares crawgs. And yet… he’s strong, stubborn, and occasionally… useful.

Personality

If you see him in the swamp, stay alert. He might charge you… or charge into trouble and drag you with him. His loyalty is… flexible. But if Blurg decides you’re on his side, he’ll fight with the fury of a storm.

• Chaotic: Always gets into trouble, often dragging others with him.
• Competitive: Loves to one-up me at everything—even if it’s just who can bonk a log the hardest.
• Brave (sometimes too brave): Doesn’t always think before charging, but rarely backs down from a fight.
• Cousinly Rival: Drives me crazy, but deep down I… respect him a little. grumpy croak

Favorite Activities

• Ramming trolls with his head.
• Stealing snacks from Blurrg (and sometimes Blurrgina).
• Randomly jumping into fights or PVP just to prove he’s the bigger Bloodbeast.
• Yelling… a lot. Usually at nothing in particular.
