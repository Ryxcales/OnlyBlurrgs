About Blurgette the Bashful (acording to Blurrg):

Hnnnn… Blurgette… little softie… tiny paws, tiny eyes, tiny… everything. croak
She was just a baby when we found her, all scared and wobbly. I… I tried to protect her. Headbutts, sniff-checks, a few warning growls… she didn’t know what to think at first. croak

Personality

When the little crawglings play, she’s always there… gently, quietly, helping them. I watch, I protect… and I think: this is why I fight, why we all fight. determined croak

• Shy & gentle: Takes time to warm up to strangers but trusts those who show her kindness.
• Thoughtful: Always notices little things, like a hidden puddle, a scared crawgling, or a shiny stone.
• Admirer: Looks up to Blurrgina, learning from her strength, grace, and cunning.
• Nice & caring: Quick to help crawgs in need, but not usually the first to charge into battle.

Favorite Activities

• Collecting interesting rocks and shiny objects from the swamp.
• Following Blurrgina around, trying to mimic her graceful movements.
• Curling up in cozy spots when the swamp gets loud or busy.
• Observing Blurrg’s antics with amused curiosity.
